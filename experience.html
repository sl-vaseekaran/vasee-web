Research Publications

Design and Analysis of Automated Film Roll Cutter

July 2024

This research focuses on the design and analysis of an automated film roll cutting mechanism. The system integrates mechanical components with precision control systems to achieve efficient and accurate cutting operations. Performance evaluation demonstrated 70% operational efficiency with optimized cutting parameters for various film dimensions.

Design of Buffer Tank in Heat Pump System

August 2023

This paper presents the design and experimental assessment of a buffer tank for heat pump systems. The research includes Finite Element Analysis (FEA) of the tank under varying pressure conditions and mesh densities. The design incorporates photovoltaic-thermal collectors to enhance heat retention, significantly reducing energy consumption of the electric heater in the heat pump system.

A Study of Sudden Discomfort of Heart Patients by Advancing the Existing Wheelchairs

July 2023

This research addresses the critical needs of elderly and heart patients who use manual wheelchairs. The study examines how individualized wheelchair design can significantly improve posture, mobility, and overall quality of life. The research identifies key factors contributing to discomfort and proposes innovative solutions to enhance wheelchair technology for better patient outcomes and reduced caregiver dependency.

Design and Development of Wall-E Robot

Engineered a functional Wall-E inspired robot through comprehensive CAD design in Autodesk Fusion 360 and slicing optimization with Ultimaker-Cura. The development process encompassed precision 3D printing of structural components followed by systematic integration of electronic systems. The resulting autonomous robot successfully demonstrates line-following capabilities and obstacle avoidance through integrated sensor arrays and programmed control algorithms.
